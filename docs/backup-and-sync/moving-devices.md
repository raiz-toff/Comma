# Moving Between Devices

Moving your data to a new phone, or running it on a phone and a laptop at once, is one task with one recommended answer: connect both devices to Cloud Sync with the same Google account and let your data flow across automatically. The full walkthrough — new phone, phone and laptop together, and the backup-file alternative — lives in the how-to guide.
